ARE YOU A GODESS?


Answer the following questions as honestly as you can. Note down all the letters in brackets after each answer that you feel most reflects you. Sometimes the same letters will appear after two different answers, but this does not matter.

QUESTIONS

1 When you look at yourself naked in the mirror, what do you really see?

a A voluptuous beauty (J, K)

b A slim, girlish body (A, C, D, E)

c A gorgeous, sexy babe (A, J)

d A strong, fit body (B, C, D)

e A fairly ordinary body (B, E)

2 When you were a teenager at school, who were you more popular with?

a Boys (C, J, K)

b Other girls (B, E)

c About equal (A, D)

3 Do you feel you have a masculine side?

a Yes (B, C, D, I)

b No (A, E, F, G, H, J)

4 If you were going to be cast in a role for a film, which would you play best?

a The romantic, glamorous lead (K)

b The lovable girlish character (D, E)

c The scheming manipulator (D, E, H)

d The intrepid adventurer (B, C, I)

e The mother figure (F, G)

f The clever career woman (B, D)

g The sexpot (A, F, G, J)

h The helpless waif (A, E)

5 If you wanted to get your own way with a man, which strategy would you adopt?

a Burst into genuine, heartfelt tears (A, E, J)

b Work out exactly what his problem was, and devise a clever argument to counter his (B, F, I, H)

c Shout, scream, rant and rave (F, A)

d You almost always get your own way (C, E, J, K)

6 Would you rather die than be seen out in public without make-up and clean hair?

s Yes (A, E, F, H, J)

b No (B, C, D, G, I)

7 How do you relate to men?

a Men are some of my best friends, as well as lovers (A, B, C, D, K)

b I find them confusing, but I need to have one around (J)

c I need a man to take the lead (E, J)

d I tend to relate to them on a sexual level (G, J)

e I really enjoy men's company. I've always been one of the lads (C, D)

8 What would you most like to be remembered for?

a My magnetic effect on men (K)

b My professional achievements (B, C, D)

c My unpredictability and creativeness (D)

d My warm, generous, humorous personality (A, E)

e My sporting/dancing achievements (B, C)

f My beauty (J, K)

g My caring personality (F, G)

h My wisdom (H, I)

9 What's your biggest fear?

a Losing control (A, B, C, E)

b Not having a man around (J, K)

c Being bored (A, B, C, D)

d Losing my looks (J, K)

10 Which group of characters in the public eye do you most admire and identify with?

a Kate Moss, Courteney Cox (A)

b Kate Winslet, Cameron Diaz (A)

c Diane Keaton, Gillian Anderson (B)

d Jamie Lee Curtis, Sigourney Weaver (C)

e Demi Moore, Madonna (D)

f Goldie Hawn, Felicity Kendal, MegRyan (E)

g Sophia Loren (F)

h Susan Sarandon, Ellen Barkin (G)

i Catherine Deneuve, Grace Kelly (H)

j Michelle Pfeiffer, Gwyneth Paltrow (I)

k Pamela Anderson, Jane Fonda as Barbarella (J)

SCORING


Count up how many times each letter or group of letters appears in your score. The more frequently each letter or group of letters occurs, the more similar you are to each goddess, or mortal. Check below to see where your true goddess or mortal nature lies.

MOSTLY As

You are Hebe, the goddess of youth. Your natural build tends to be slim and girlish. You may have a tendency to be obsessed with diets. You are charming and disarming, and tend to be liked more by the opposite sex than your own.

MOSTLY Bs

You are Athena, battle goddess of education and science. You are fit and alluring, the cleverest of all and the most powerful. On the downside, you can be selfish and preoccupied with your own ideas. You would make a very bad enemy.

MOSTLY Cs

You are Artemis, goddess of wild things, dance and hunting. You are a tomboy woman. You are independent, clever and fit. You have a directness and an honesty about you that is very appealing.

MOSTLY Ds

You are Hermes, goddess of new beginnings, motion and commerce. You are an intellectual; very aware of what's going on around you. You love change. At your worst, you will lie quite happily, and stir up trouble for others if it amuses you or suits your purpose.

MOSTLY Es

You are Eros, cupid goddess of sweethearts. You are so pretty and cute you can get away with quite a lot of mischief. You are not malicious but playful, and great fun to be with. You can also be stubborn and obstinate.

MOSTLY Fs

You are a Fire Diva. Your nature is fiery and unpredictable and you like it that way.

MOSTLY Gs

You are an Earth Diva. You are down to earth and passionate in a very natural way. You can reduce men to jelly.

MOSTLY Hs

You are an Ice Diva. You are so cool, people find it hard to get close to you. Once you find your man, you will melt in front of him once he has passed the tests you have set for him.

MOSTLY Is

You are an Air Diva. You are a very elusive woman; men who meet you may feel that it could take a lifetime to tease out all your possibilities.

MOSTLY Js

You are Helen of Troy, a mere mortal. But what a babe. You are beautiful and value this asset highly. You are an upfront, in-your-face kind of girl. Men love you, but you are not the most popular girl on the block with your own sex.

A MIX OF F, G, H, I, J & K

You are the Ultimate Diva, Aphrodite, goddess of love and beauty. You are a naturally sexy and uninhibited person. You do have a very strong personality - once you have made up your mind about something, you are unlikely to change.
