ARE YOU A GOD?


Answer the following questions as honestly as you can. Note down the letters in brackets after each answer that you feel most reflects you. Sometimes the same letters will appear after two different answers, but this does not matter.

QUESTIONS

1 Get your kit off and take a look at yourself in the mirror, and what do you honestly see?

a A devastatingly handsome fellow (A, C)

b A fit, toned and strong body (B, F, H)

c The latest version of whichever image I am now portraying (D)

d A boyish body (E, G)

e Started off well, but slowly losing it (A)

2 How do women see you?

a As a wise, fair and handsome man (C)

b As an irresistible charmer (A, E, F, G)

c They admire my physical strength (B)

d As a hero/action man (F, H)

e They never really know me (D)

3 Do you feel you have a feminine side?

a Yes ( A, D, E, G)

b No (B, C, F, H)

4 Do people seek your advice?

a Yes, often (A, C, D, H)

b Not really (B, E, F, G)

5 Which statement most reflects you?

a I believe in playing fair (C, F, G)

b In life you play to win, whatever (D, H)

c I'm very good at persuading other people to do what I want them to do (A, D, E)

d I sulk when things don't go my way (E, F)

e I like to be the centre of attention (B, E)

6 What is your attitude towards women?

a They are there for my pleasure (A, D)

b I love them but cannot understand them (B, F, G)

c I feel they are friends as well as lovers (A, H)

d I enjoy spending time with women more than I do with men (A, E, G)

7 Which statement applies best to you?

a I am very competitive and prefer to lead (B, F)

b I am very creative and individualistic (A, D, H)

c I go with the flow and work best alongside others (E, G)

d I prefer to keep my own counsel and I trust my own judgment (C)

8 What would you most like to be remembered for?

a My professional achievements (D)

b My sex appeal and my romantic conquests (E, G)

c My sporting or action adventures (B, F, H)

d For being a loving family man (A, H)

e My ability to surprise and amuse others (D)

f My wisdom and fairness (C)

9 You know your body best. How do you most like to spend your spare time?

a Playing sport and making love energetically (A, B, C, F, G, H)

b Watching sport, propping up a bar, sitting at my computer, reading, painting/drawing, and making love with a minimum of energy (A, C, D, E)

10 Which group of characters in the public eye do you most admire and identify with?

a Jack Nicholson, Michael Douglas, Gérard Depardieu, Gary Oldman (A)

b Richard Gere, John Travolta, Patrick Swayze, Tom Jones (A)

c Arnold Schwarzenegger, Sly Stallone, the Gladiators (B)

d Paul Newman, Robert Redford, Michael Jordan (C)

e David Bowie, Julian Clary, Michael Jackson, Steve Coogan (D)

f Tom Hanks, Steve Martin, Robin Williams, Dudley Moore (E)

g Tom Cruise, Harrison Ford, Mel Gibson, Kevin Costner, Pierce Brosnan (F)

h Andre Agassi, Michael Jordan, Michael Owen (F)

i George Clooney, Johnny Depp, Brad Pitt, Kiefer Sutherland (F)

j Leonardo DiCaprio, Ewan McGregor, Keanu Reeves (G)

k Harrison Ford, Denzel Washington (H)

SCORING


Count up how many times each letter or group of letters appears in your score. The more frequently each letter or group of letters occurs, the more similar you are to each god or mortal. Check below to see where your true godly or mortal nature lies.

MOSTLY As

You are Dionysus, half-mortal, the god of dance and ecstasy. Yet you are also the only god who, once he found the woman he wanted, was totally faithful. You are determined, ambitious and cagey. You are very interesting to others, subtle in your ways, and you think deeply.

MOSTLY Bs

You are Hercules, the strongest man. You enjoy pumping iron. So long as your woman believes you are god's gift, you will go to the ends of the earth for her. You feel you have to work for what you get. Your weakness is a need to be the centre of attention.

MOSTLY Cs

You are Apollo, the god of truth and justice. Not only are you damn gorgeous, you are clever, too. Naturally, you tend to be a role model and inspiration to others. You are responsible, faithful and caring.

MOSTLY Ds

You are Hermes, the god of new beginnings, motion and commerce. You have a chameleon nature. You can have a very airy-fairy side to your character, but you are always shrewd with money. Your main weakness is a tendency to stir things up.

MOSTLY Es

You are Eros, the cupid god of love. You might actually be (or have been) quite a pretty young man, and people will often think of you as cute. Because your childlike personality often comes to the fore, you can also be manipulative and mischievous.

MOSTLY Fs

You are Ares, the god of war. You are fit and take a pride in your appearance. Even if you abuse your body because of your addictive nature, you have a constitution of iron.

MOSTLY Gs

You are Adonis, a mere mortal, but a beautiful, youthful one. You cannot help but know it and women go mad for you. You are not irresponsible, and if you are fickle, it is simply because you are young.

MOSTLY Hs

You are Odysseus, another mere mortal but, again, a gorgeous and clever one. You are a swashbuckler, a risk-taker and a romantic.
